Single Page vs Multi-Page Website Options

You can choose to have your website as a single scrolling page (like this demo) where all content flows seamlessly, or as a traditional multi-page website with separate chambers.

📜 Single Page Layout

  • All content on one mechanical scrolling page
  • Smooth navigation between sections
  • Perfect for mobile steampunk readers
  • Quick to load and explore

📖 Multi-Page Layout

  • Separate chambers: Home, Books, About, Contact
  • More space for detailed steampunk content
  • Traditional Victorian structure
  • Ideal for extensive mechanical series

We'll help you decide which configuration works best for your steampunk brand and reader engagement goals during our consultation.
